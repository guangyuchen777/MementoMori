About the story

The board game to talk about ' it ' now.

Although we all will eventually be confronted with death, be it of our parents, family or friends, death is still a taboo. Most people don’t want to talk about it, because they see it as a sad topic they want to stay far away from. However, avoiding discussing death will leave us vulnerable and unprepared when the moment arrives. That’s why we developed a board game, Memento Mori, to help people to talk about death in a way which is natural, without the sadness and heaviness that is often associated with dying and even with a twist of humor.

How to play

The Game in Brief

All the players start at the snake head and take turns to draw life event cards. According to the life event cards, you can move a certain amount of steps forward or backwards on the gameboard. When reaching the instruction block, you need to answer a question or perform an action. If you reach a telegate block, you will go to the snake mouth directly and ‘experience death’. Then you need to start at the snake head again. Based on the exchange rules, you can use the life event cards to buy the funeral ritual cards. The object of the game is to be the first one who collects 5 different types of Funeral Ritual Cards.

Game Manuals

Design Details

The Game Board

The board path uses a death metaphor Ouroboros(self-eating snake). It is an ancient symbol to signifying infinity and the cycle of birth and death. During the game, a player will be guided to the 'snake mouth' by chance and experience a 'death' with the help of Augmented Reality (AR), then restart the path. We aim to let the player understand that death can sometimes be unexpected but it doesn't necessarily mean the end.

Play Cards

We believe that death is part of life. Without living, you can never die. Therefore we designed life event cards and the death ritual cards in this game. The players can use Life event cards to collect their funeral ritual cards. The winner is the one who first collect 5 funeral ritual cards.

AR app

At the end of the game, the players can use the smartphone to scan the death ritual cards they collect and see a visualization of a 'funeral' they just won. The AR function can deepen the immersion feeling of the death experience.
